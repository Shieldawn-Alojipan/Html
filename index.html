Diamond Jozu

Jozu is a big, muscular, heavily-armored and dark-skinned man. The dark armor he wears and his hair style are very similar to that of an Aztec warrior, with red-bolted darl shoulder pads. The front and back portions of his armor are connected by a metal strap that goes over both his collarbones. Jozu has a long face with stripe-like patterns for a beard and hairstyle. His legs are relatively thin, in comparison to his muscular arms. He appears battle-hardened and has a perpetual scowl on his face. is a gruff, fairly quiet individual who tends to point out info to his crewmates. He appears to be quite unrelenting to his enemies as he had no qualms in attempting to strike an almost-downed Crocodile a second time immediately after a first hit. Like the other commanders, he is loyal to Whitebeard and his crew.As the commander of the third division of the Whitebeard Pirates, Jozu has authority over the lower-ranking subordinates. He was one of the few men unaffected by the powerful Haki released by the Yonkou Shanks. He is very experienced and a veteran of the Whitebeard pirates from the times of Gol D. Roger. He is one of the strongest members of the Whitebeard Pirates, as shown by his position as the third division commander. During the Marineford war, Jozu was powerful enough to fight almost equally against the Shichibukai and the Marine Admirals..

Ace

Portgas D. Ace, born as Gol D. Ace and nicknamed "Fire Fist Ace", is a major character in the One Piece franchise. He is the adoptive older brother of Monkey D. Luffy and Sabo, and the son of the Pirate King Gol D. Roger and his lover Portgas D. Rouge. He was adopted by Monkey D. Garp as wished by Roger to him before his birth. Ace is the 2nd division commander of the Whitebeard Pirates and one-time captain of the Spade Pirates. During the Summit War, he was killed by Akainu in cold blood after Akainu provoked him into combat by insulting Whitebeard and his Whitebeard Pirates and trying to attack Luffy.

Vista

Vista, a prominent character in the world renowned manga and anime series One Piece, holds the esteemed position of the 5th division commander in the mighty Whitebeard Pirates. As the right-hand man to Whitebeard himself, Vista's unwavering loyalty and exceptional swordsmanship make him a force to be reckoned with in the treacherous seas of the Grand Line.One of the defining traits of Vista is his unyielding loyalty to Whitebeard, the captain of the Whitebeard Pirates. This loyalty stems not only from a sense of duty but also from a deep bond of respect and admiration. Vista recognizes the superior strength and charisma of Whitebeard, and he willingly stands by the captain's side, ready to defend the crew and their ambitions with everything he has.In terms of combat prowess, Vista stands head and shoulders above most swordsmen in the One Piece universe. As a master of the sword, his skills are honed to perfection, making him a formidable adversary for anyone who dares to cross swords with him. Vista's mastery over his weapon allows him to execute devastating attacks with precision and speed, effortlessly slicing through both foes and obstacles in his path.It is noteworthy to analyze the theoretical aspects of Vista's character, which significantly complement his role as the 5th division commander. The number five has a symbolic meaning in many cultures, representing strength, resilience, and adaptability. Vista exemplifies these qualities as he leads the 5th division and ensures the smooth functioning of the Whitebeard Pirates' hierarchy.Moreover, Vista's role as commander highlights the importance of strategic planning and tactical execution within the Whitebeard Pirates..

Kozuki Oden

The Whitebeard Pirates were once one of the strongest pirate crews in the world, as their late captain, Edward Newgate, better known as "Whitebeard", was one of the Four Emperors and the only man to have ever been a match for the Pirate King, Gol D. Roger, in a fight.[3] Whitebeard's crew was segmented into divisions, with each led by a powerful division commander. The crew also had multiple subordinate crews operating in the New World, allowing the pirate crew to function as a massive pirate fleet. In addition, they controlled several territories throughout the New World, including Fish-Man Island.Two years before the current storyline, the Whitebeard Pirates and many of their subordinate crews engaged Marine Headquarters in the legendary Summit War of Marineford over the fate of the crew's second division commander, Portgas D. Ace. Unfortunately, both Ace and Whitebeard met their untimely ends; the latter having been murdered by the Blackbeard Pirates led by Marshall D. Teach aka "Blackbeard", who had betrayed the crew earlier on. Following this, the Whitebeard Pirates lost much of their power and influence in the New World, and many of their former territories were conquered by Blackbeard. A year later, the remaining members of the crew, led by first division commander Marco "the Phoenix", engaged the Blackbeard Pirates' full forces in the Payback War, which ended with their brutal defeat, thus leading to them disbanding and Blackbeard becoming Whitebeard's replacement within the Four Emperors..
